    target-column auto-detection, binary encoding of protected attrs, ordinal
    encoding of other categoricals, NaN row dropping, and df_clean preservation.

  test_intersectional

    ascending sort order (worst first), MIN_GROUP_SIZE filtering, and EOpD
    population when y_pred is supplied.

  test_mitigator
    Verifies AIF360 Reweighing: weight array length, positivity, directional
    correctness (unprivileged+positive upweighted vs privileged+positive),
    group_weight_map population, and sum(weights) ≈ N normalisation.

  test_provenance
    Verifies bias root-cause detection: high-MI proxy triggers feature_bias,
    negligible proxy does not, confidence stays in [0,1], and evidence /
    recommended_fixes lists are non-empty.

  test_proxy_scanner
